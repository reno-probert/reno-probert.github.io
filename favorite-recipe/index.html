Dairy Free Waffles

A variation of my father-in-law's famous waffle recipe using all dairy free ingredients. A dairy free breakfast without sacrificing taste!

>

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • sprinkle of cinnamon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups oat milk (or any dairy-free milk or fruit juice)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
