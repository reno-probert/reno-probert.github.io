Granola

❤❤❤❤❤

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped nuts and seeds (pumpkin, flax, sunflower, etc.)
  • 8 cups oats
  • ½ cup canola oil
  • ½ cup honey
  • 2 cups dried fruit (pineapple, cranberries, etc.)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix together oats and chopped nuts/seeds and place in single layer on baking sheets.
  3. Mix together oil and honey.
  4. Toss honey and oil mixture over oats and seeds/nuts.
  5. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally until edges are brown.
  6. Remove from oven and add fruit.
