Dairy Free Chocolate Brownie Cookies

❤❤❤❤❤

  • Servings: 2 Dozen Cookies
  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 9 minutes

Ingredients

  • ¾ cups crisco
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • ⅔ cups cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup dairy free chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, beat crisco and sugar until light and creamy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time until mixed, then add vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl sift flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix.
  6. Mix in chocolate chips.
  7. Spooon tablespoon sized portions onto a wax paper covered baking sheet.
  8. Lightly grease the waffle iron with a brush of oil.
  9. Cook 9 minutes
  10. Remove from oven and let cool on a wire rack.
