Pacific Rhododendron Rhododendron macrophyllum

Alternate Names

The Pacific rhododendron is also known as:

  • California rosebay
  • Coast rhododendron
  • California rhododendron

Habitat Range

Pacific rhododendron can be found in the Pacific Northwest East of the Cascade Mountain Range from southern British Columbia to Northern California.

Preferred Climate

Dry forested climates or edges of forests, grows particularly well in the rain shadow created by the Olympic Mountains.

Size Range

Typically grows between 3 to 15 feet tall, but can reach heights of up to 26 feet in shaded conditions.

Climate Benefits

  • Provides habitat and food for pollinators.
  • Root system helps prevent soil erosion.
  • Relatively low water usage.
